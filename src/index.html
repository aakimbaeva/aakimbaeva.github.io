Boston Crimes Dataset

The project analyzed crime data from the Boston Police Department to predict specific types of crimes. The dataset covers incidents from June 14, 2015, to September 3, 2018, detailing variables like location, time, and day of the week. Tasks included data loading, cleaning, exploratory analysis, correlation assessment, and machine learning for prediction. It was a collaborative effort among myself and two classmates for a Data Engineering course.

Credit Scoring Project

The project aimed to compare credit scoring models for accuracy in evaluating applicants' credit scores. Using a dataset of 26,962 business loans from a bank for 2016-2017, including 44 variables covering company information, financial data, and socio-demographic features, the study focused on distinguishing between default and non-default cases. Tasks included data loading, cleaning, exploratory analysis, correlation assessment, and comparison of several prediction models. This collaborative effort was undertaken for a Data Mining course with a classmate.
